Fried Chicken Recipe

This Fried Chicken Recipe will lead to you having the juiciest and crispy-est Friday night of your life!

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken
  • 2 Cups of Buttermilk
  • 1 Teaspoon of Garlic Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Onion Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Poulty Seasoning
  • 1 Teaspoon of Celery Seeds
  • Vegitable Oil for Deep Frying
  • 2 Cups Kikkoman Tempura Batter Mix

Directions

  1. Wash chicken and pat it dry.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together buttermilk, garlic powder, onion powder, poultry seasoning, and celery seeds.
  3. Place chicken in the mixture and refridgerate overnight.
  4. Remove Chicken from fridge and drain excess buttermilk.
  5. Place chicken in bag with batter mix. Shake!
  6. Heat oil to 350 degrees F and put the chicken in.
  7. Turn pieces over every 3 minutes until brown on all sides
  8. Enjoy!
