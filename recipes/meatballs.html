Meatballs

These meatballs are the perfect addition to any pasta you have the desire to cook!

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds Ground Beef
  • 1 1/2 Cups Fresh Bread Crumbs
  • 1/4 Cups Chopped Onion
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 18oz. Bottle BBQ sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, bread, onion, milk, salt and eggs. Shape into little meatballs, about 1 inch in size. Place the meatballs into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Remove
  4. Pour BBQ sauce over meatballs then cook additional 35 minutes.
